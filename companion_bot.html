Companion Bot

Overview

The Companion Bot is a distinctive and compassionate chatbot designed to provide emotional support and inform users about available resources on campus to meet their needs. Here are the key features:

Social Interaction

  • The chatbot fosters social interaction by engaging users in friendly and meaningful conversations.
  • It initiates chats, shares interesting topics, and provides companionship to help users feel content.

Early Intervention

  • The chatbot employs proactive monitoring to detect early signs of stress, anxiety, or emotional distress in users that may lead to academic declines and mental health issues.
  • By analyzing user interactions and mood indicators, it can identify when someone might benefit from additional support or resources.

Mindful Well-Being

  • The chatbot offers mental health support by providing a safe and non-judgmental space for users to discuss their feelings and concerns.
  • It shares helpful resources, coping strategies, and encourages users to seek help when needed.
Currently, the Companion Bot supports Social Interaction only. It operates on our earlier technology known as Emora State Transition Dialogue Manager. However, we have plans to transition to the latest technology powered by large language models in the near future.
