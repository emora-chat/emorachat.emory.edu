Assistant Bot

Overview

The Assistant Bot aims to serve as a personal assistant and facilitate communication among people in the Emory community. Here are the key features:

Appointment Scheduling

  • The chatbot simplifies the process of scheduling appointments or meetings between students, faculty, staff, and other members of the Emory community.
  • Users can check the availability of others, propose meeting times, and receive confirmation or rescheduling notifications.

Automatic Answering

  • Any user can train the chatbot in providing answers to frequently asked questions from other users (e.g., When are your office hours?).
  • This not only saves time for the user providing the training by avoiding the need to repeatedly answer the same questions but also ensures that other users receive quick through the chatbot.

Feedback Collection

  • The chatbot facilitates feedback collection from specific groups (e.g., classroom, club) through surveys and questionnaires.
  • Users can take part in surveys related to their course experiences (can be anonymous) or provide feedback on events, helping those groups gather valuable insights for improvement.

Collaborator Discovery

  • Users can search for potential classmates to assist with their course materials or team members for group projects by specifying their needs and interests.
  • The chatbot matches users with relevant expertise and facilitates connections for collaborative opportunities, promoting innovation and teamwork.

Community Building

  • The chatbot encourages social interaction and community building by helping users find others who share similar interests.
  • Users can explore profiles and connect with fellow students or colleagues who have common hobbies, research interests, or extracurricular activities.
Currently, the Assistant Bot supports Appointment Scheduling only, and the scheduling is available exclusively to Emora Chat users. Moreover, the chatboot will send you push notifications to keep you informed about requests and confirmations from other users.

Benefits

Our Personal Assistant Chatbot is a valuable asset for the Emory community, enhancing communication, collaboration, and interaction among students, faculty, and staff. It streamlines various aspects contributes to a more connected and informed campus environment. Its key benefits can be summarized as follows:

  • Enhances communication and collaboration among Emory community members.
  • Reduces administrative workload by automating common tasks.
  • Promotes community engagement and social connections.
  • Collects valuable feedback for continuous improvement.

To the best of our knowledge, this is the first chatbot that serves as a mediator, enabling simultaneous communication among multiple users.

Sample Dialogue

Here is an example dialogue demonstrating how two users can schedule an appointment using this chatbot:

Ellie (E) and Chatbot (C)

  • E: Hello
  • C: Before we begin, please provide your full name.
  • E: Ellie Paek
  • C: Thank you Ellie Paek. Welcome! How should I call you?
  • E: Call me Ellie
  • C: Nice to meet you, Ellie. So, how's it going, Ellie?
  • E: Can I make an appointment with Dr. Choi?
  • C: Okay! Please tell me your first choice for a meeting day and time with Jinho Choi.
  • E: Tomorrow at 9 AM
  • C: Ok, I think I got it. Is this what you meant? 09/11/2023 (09:00 AM) to 09/11/2023 (10:00 AM)
  • E: Yes
  • C: Awesome, I'll see if they are free. And where do you want to meet with Jinho Choi?
  • E: In his office
  • C: Ok, I will mark the location as In his office. Would you like to include another alternative time to meet?
  • E: Not for now
  • C: Understood, that's it. Please tell me your reason for wanting to meet with Jinho Choi.
  • E: I'd like to talk about CS 329.
  • C: Thank you! I'll send off your request:
    • Meeting, James Finch and Jinho Choi: 09/11/2023 (09:00 AM) to 09/11/2023 (10:00 AM) at In his office. (Discussing CS 329)
  • E: Thanks
  • C: Of course! It's my pleasure.

Jinho (J) and Chatbot (C)

  • C: Hello! So, how's it going, Jinho?
  • J: Great, any updates for me?
  • C: You have 1 message in your appointment inbox. Would you like to respond to it now?
  • J: Sure
  • C: On 09/10/2023 (06:26 PM), Ellie Paek proposed the following new event:
    • Meeting, Ellie Paek and Jinho Choi: 09/11/2023 (09:00 AM) to 09/11/2023 (10:00 AM) at In his office. (Discussing CS 329)
         Does that work for you?
  • J: Yes, that works for me
  • C: Awesome, I'll let Ellie know!
  • J: Great
  • C: There are no new requests in your inbox.

Ellie (E) and Chatbot (C)

  • E: Anything new?
  • C: You have 1 message in your appointment inbox. Would you like to respond to it now?
  • E: Yes
  • C: Good news! Jinho Choi replied that 09/11/2023 (09:00 AM) to 09/11/2023 (10:00 AM) is a good time to meet about "Discussing CS 329". Your appointment has been confirmed.
  • E: Awesome
  • C: There are no new requests in your inbox.
  • E: Thanks
