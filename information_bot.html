Information Bot

Overview

The Information Bot aims to meet the specific needs of the Emory community by providing accurate and timely information. Here are the key features:

General FAQ

  • The chatbot maintains an extensive repository of frequently asked questions (FAQs) from various sources relevant to Emory University.
  • Users can ask common questions about about admissions, financial aid, campus facilities, student services, and more.

Academic Inquiry

  • The chatbot covers a range of topics, including majors, minors, academic requirements, and course details and availability.
  • It can answer questions about specific academic programs and degrees, and help students explore different academic paths and career opportunities.

News & Events

  • Stay up-to-date with the latest news, announcements, and events happening in the Emory community.
  • Users can inquire about upcoming seminars, workshops, campus news, social events, and special announcements happening at or related to Emory University.

Campus Activities

  • Discover real-time information about live activities, such as sports events, art performances, or cultural gatherings, taking place on the Emory campus.
  • Users can inquire about activity schedules, ticketing information, and venue details.

Student Organizations

  • Engage with student clubs and organizations at Emory University.
  • Users can explore a directory of student clubs, including their purposes, meeting times, and contact details. The chatbot can assist in joining clubs or finding ways to get involved in their activities.
Currently, the Information Bot supports General FAQs only, encompassing questions that pertain to all contents found in the Emory Resources created for new students at Emory College of Arts and Sciences.

ChatGPT for Emory

Our chatbot serves as a valuable and specialized resource for the Emory community, offering a unique advantage over general AI models like ChatGPT by delivering precise and Emory-specific information along with credible sources. It enhances the university's ability to provide accurate and tailored support to its students, faculty, and staff. Its key advantages over ChatGPT can be summarized as follows:

  • Emory-Specific Knowledge: The Emory University Information Bot is specifically trained and tailored to provide answers to questions that are exclusive to the Emory University environment. It possesses specialized knowledge about the university's policies, procedures, and resources, which ChatGPT might not have.
  • Accuracy and Relevance: While ChatGPT is a general AI model, the Emory chatbot is designed to ensure the accuracy and relevance of information specific to Emory University. Users can trust that the responses are reliable and directly related to their needs.
  • Providing Sources: In addition to answering questions, the Emory chatbot goes a step further by providing users with sources of information. This helps users verify the information and access official Emory University websites and resources for more details.
