Emora Chat Demos

Mobile Usage

Emora Chat has implemented a simple UI. Chat with Emora like you would using other messaging apps. You can use the report function to the side of the bubble to report whether you liked or disliked the response.

Installation

Emora chat is available both on the App Store and Google Play store. We have highlighted how to install the app through the App Store, but the process would be similar through Google Play Store. To install Emora chat on your device go here.

Server Down

While it is unlikely that you will experience this issue, it may happen that our servers may go down. If this happens to you, you will not be able to log in, send messages or view your previous messages until the server is back up. We'll be aware when our servers are down so while we work on a fix, please wait and simply try to log in at a later time.

Terms of Service

In order to use Emorachat, you must accept the terms of service. If you ever want to read the terms of service again after accepting you can find them by following this demo.
